## Maintenance Guide

### 1. Update Profile & Socials
Modify `_config.yml`. To enable social links in the future, simply remove the `#` comment symbol.

### 2. Update Homepage Content
Edit `index.md` to update "About Me", "Research Interests", and "News".

### 3. Update Publications
1. Place the teaser image in `assets/img/`.
2. Update the paper details in `_data/publications.yml`.

### 4. Update Awards
Modify `_includes/selected_awards.md` to list your latest achievements.

### 5. Change Avatar & Icons
Replace `avatar.png` and `favicon.png` in `assets/img/`.
